This is not Huang Hai’s autobiography. It is a friend’s view, written five years after they met. The perspective is preserved; its figures and interpretations remain time-bound reporting, not independently verified fact.

01

一次重逢A REUNION

作者与黄海相识于2020年的杭州。五年后再见，城市里的旧友看到的是一个明显黑瘦、留起长发、在高原和城市之间往返的人。报道从这次重逢开始，而不是从一个被设计好的传奇开始。

The writer first met Huang Hai in Hangzhou in 2020. Five years later, the reunion begins with visible change: thinner, darker from the sun, moving between plateau and city.

02

一辆车与很多城市ONE CAR, MANY CITIES

报道回望了北京、杭州和2021年开始的自驾。它把旅行理解为行动，而不是答案：黄海在许多地方想过停留，最后让他返回理塘的是与具体人的关系。

Beijing, Hangzhou and a long drive across China form the road into the story. Travel is presented as action rather than an answer; relationships, not scenery alone, bring him back.

03

牧场的代价THE COST OF A PASTURE

牦牛章节没有回避损失、经验不足和没有闭合的商业模式。报道中的牛群数量、投入和收入来源属于作者在2025年发布时的记录，仍需按日期、所有权和经营口径分别核验。

The pasture chapter does not hide loss, inexperience or an unfinished business model. Herd and financial figures belong to the article’s 2025 reporting frame and require independent verification.

04

旷野不是答案OPEN COUNTRY IS NOT AN ANSWER

文章最后回到城市里的告别。所谓旷野，并不是对城市生活的否定，而是作者对选择、有限时间和不同人生路径的理解。

The story ends back in the city. Open country is not a rejection of urban life, but the writer’s way of thinking about choice, finite time and divergent paths.
