高原四季与风物 / PLATEAU SERIES

我想把高原的四季，
原原本本地带到城市里。I WANT TO BRING THE PLATEAU’S SEASONS
TO THE CITY, AS THEY ARE.

我分享的不是抽象的“远方”。它有松针、雪水、草场、牦牛和一段段真实的等待。

The distant horizon is not an abstraction. It has pine needles, snowmelt, pasture, yaks—and real seasons of waiting.

01 / SEASONS OF THE PLATEAU

高原四季

雪、草场、风雨与牛群，构成了牧场一年真正的时间表。

Snow, grass, weather and herd make the pasture’s real calendar.

02 / MATSUTAKE SEASON

松茸季

等雨水、等菌子、也等合适的冷链。每一朵松茸都先属于山林。

Waiting for rain, mushrooms and the right cold chain. Every matsutake belongs first to the forest.

03 / CORDYCEPS SEASON

虫草季

从营地到收集，从高原天气到一日的行程，这是每年短暂而紧凑的季节。

From camp to collection, weather to a day’s journey: a brief and concentrated season.

04 / THINGS OF THE MOUNTAIN

山中风物

青稞、沙棘、黄芪与草木，带着各自的成熟时间走向城市。

Barley, sea buckthorn, astragalus and wild plants reach the city in their own time.
