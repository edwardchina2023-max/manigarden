麦尼海塘 / FIELD ARCHIVE

高原不是一种滤镜。
它有自己的天气、节律与生命。THE PLATEAU IS NOT A FILTER.
IT HAS ITS OWN WEATHER, RHYTHM AND LIFE.

这些照片和影片来自黄海的真实记录。它们按主题整理，不替任何人或一处地方概括全部经验。

These photographs and films come from Huang Hai’s lived record. Grouped by theme, they never claim to represent a whole place or people.

01 / SEASONS OF THE PASTURE

02 / DAYS WITH HUANG HAI

黄海的日常DAYS WITH HUANG HAI

走路、吃饭、看云、和朋友相处；牧场之外，生活仍是生活。

Walking, eating, watching clouds, being with friends—the rest of life remains life.

营地、天气、等待与收集，组成高原上短暂而紧凑的一个季节。
